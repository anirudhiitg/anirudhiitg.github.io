How It's Made

Grown at our Hanumangarh farm

2

Hand-picked at peak freshness

3

Our Story

A farm in Hanumangarh, a habit for life

"We grow it, we make it, we use it every day — and now we want to share it with you."

We have a farm in Hanumangarh, Rajasthan where we grow Moringa and Amla. The powder is made entirely in-house — from harvesting to drying to grinding to packing. No outsourcing, no commercial processing.

But the farm is only half the story. We genuinely live by what we grow. Moringa powder with curd is our daily protein. A kitchen garden at home keeps us connected to fresh produce. And cooking everything from scratch is the habit that ties it all together.

We started Premnagar Farms to share both — the pure products we make, and the simple habits that make a real difference. Because good health isn't a product. It's a practice.
