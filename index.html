How It's Made

From our Hanumangarh farm to your kitchen

Everything happens at our farm in Hanumangarh, Rajasthan — growing, drying, grinding, and packing. No outsourcing, no middlemen.

1

Grown at our Hanumangarh farm

Moringa trees and Amla plants cultivated on our own land in Hanumangarh, Rajasthan — naturally, without pesticides.

2

Hand-picked at peak freshness

Leaves and fruits picked at optimal maturity to lock in maximum nutrients.

3

Sun-dried slowly

Dried in shade or mild sun at the farm to preserve colour, aroma, and nutritional value.

4

Ground & packed in-house

Finely powdered and packed right at the farm in small batches — no external processing facility, no warehousing.

Our Story

A farm in Hanumangarh, a habit for life

"We grow it, we make it, we use it every day — and now we want to share it with you."

We have a farm in Hanumangarh, Rajasthan where we grow Moringa and Amla. The powder is made entirely in-house — from harvesting to drying to grinding to packing. No outsourcing, no commercial processing.

But the farm is only half the story. We genuinely live by what we grow. Moringa powder with curd is our daily protein. A kitchen garden at home keeps us connected to fresh produce. And cooking everything from scratch is the habit that ties it all together.

We started The Green Habit Farm to share both — the pure products we make, and the simple habits that make a real difference. Because good health isn't a product. It's a practice.
