Why I started growing Moringa in my kitchen garden

March 2025  ·  4 min read  ·  Kitchen Garden

← Back to Healthy Habits

It started with a single Moringa seedling my neighbour gave me. I planted it in a corner of my yard, half-expecting it to wither like everything else I'd tried to grow. Three months later, it was taller than me.

Why Moringa?

I'd been reading about Moringa's nutritional profile — more iron than spinach, more vitamin C than oranges, complete amino acids. But reading about something and actually growing it are two very different experiences.

"When you grow your own food, you stop treating it like a supplement. It becomes a relationship."
